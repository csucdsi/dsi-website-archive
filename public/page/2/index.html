ASA DataFest 2020 @ Chico State - April 17-19

By Robin Donatello on November 7, 2019

Save the Date - April 17-19, 2020! In 2019 Chico State’s ASA DataFest tripled the participation from our first year. DataFest 2020 is set to break (our) records! What is the ASA DataFest? ASA DataFest is a data hackathon for undergraduate students, sponsored by the American Statistical Association and founded at UCLA, in 2011.

About us

The Chico State Data Science Initative strives to foster a data-empowered society working within a culture of equity-minded open science. We do so by connecting people to opportunities to learn new skills and engage in impactful research across the spectrum of disciplines.

What we do

  • Curriculum Development
  • Professional Data Skills Training
  • Data Wrangling/Analysis
  • Interactive Dashboard Storytelling
  • Student Internships
  • Project/Thesis Consulting
